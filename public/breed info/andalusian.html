Horse Breeds 101

Andalusians

Typical Uses and Temperament

Common uses for Andalusians include Dressage, Equitation and Western Pleasure. Although they are suitable for many sports due to their smart, sensitive and docile nature.

Typical Colors

Gray or Bay

Average Height

Between 15.2 and 16.2 hands

Region of Origin

Spain

History

Carthusian monks began breeding in the middle ages. Studs were later founded at different monastaries. The bloodlines were kept pure over the years, and has developed the name PRE or the Pure Spanish Breed.
