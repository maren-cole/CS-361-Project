Horse Breeds 101

Arabian

Typical Uses and Temperament

Common uses for Arabians include Endurance, Pleasure Riding, Dressage and Jumping. They are often well suited for high intensity sports due to their smart, alert and inquisitive nature.

Typical Colors

Gray, Bay, Chestnut, Black, Roan

Average Height

14.1-15.1 hh

Region of Origin

Middle East

History

The Arabian or Arab horse is a breed of horse with historic roots on the Arabian Peninsula. With a distinctive head shape and high tail carriage, the Arabian is one of the most easily recognizable horse breeds in the world. It is also one of the oldest modern breeds.
