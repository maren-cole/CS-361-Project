Irish Sport Horse

Typical Uses and Temperament

Irish Sport Horses excel in Jumping, Eventing and Dressage due to their naturally athletic build and smart/bold nature.

Typical Colors

Black, Brown, Bay, Chestnut, Gray, Roan

Average Height

15-17 hh

Region of Origin

Ireland

History

The Irish Sport Horse, or Irish Hunter, is an Irish breed of warmblood sporting horse, used mostly for dressage, eventing and show-jumping.
